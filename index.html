How you read

Six types of reader.
Which pattern fits you?

Most people have never named this. But the moment they see it, they recognize themselves immediately.

The Emotional Excavator

You read to feel things fully. Books that make you cry in public are not accidents — they're exactly what you came for.

The Pattern Seeker

Every book is a system to decode. You love fiction that reveals how the world actually works — beneath the surface.

The Mirror Reader

You're looking for yourself on every page. The right book doesn't tell you something new. It tells you something true.

The Atmosphere Addict

Plot is secondary. Give you prose that builds a world you can disappear into — and don't rush you out of it.

The Complexity Craver

You distrust tidy endings. The books you love don't resolve — they open up. You're comfortable with what doesn't close.

The Escape Artist

Reading is the one place nothing else exists. You're not in it for insight. You're in it to leave — fully and completely.
