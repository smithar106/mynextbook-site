Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026 · Last updated: May 2, 2026

My Next Book ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the My Next Book mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Anonymous usage data: When you first open the App, an anonymous session is created. No name, email address, or personal identifier is required to use the basic features of the App.
  • Style interaction data: We collect your in-app interactions — swipes (like, skip, save), item views, dwell time, and mood selections. This data is used exclusively to power your personal style recommendations.
  • Device information: We may collect basic device identifiers, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic and support purposes.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to My Next Book Pro, purchase transactions are processed by Apple's App Store. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Power and improve your personalised fashion feed
  • Build and update your Style DNA profile in real time
  • Improve the accuracy of our recommendation engine over time
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance
  • Verify your subscription status and provide Pro features

We do not sell your data. We do not use your style data for advertising purposes beyond the My Next Book App itself.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your interaction data and style profile are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Anonymous user sessions are tied to a device-level identifier, not a personal account.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Apple App Store — subscription and payment processing (apple.com/legal/privacy)
  • ASOS / RapidAPI — product catalog data (product images and details only; no user data is shared)

5. Data Retention

Your style interaction data and preferences are retained as long as your anonymous session is active. If you uninstall the App, your anonymous session data may be retained on our servers for up to 12 months before deletion. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@mynextbook.me.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@mynextbook.me.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
