Frequently Asked Questions

How do recommendations work?

My Next Book builds a taste profile based on books you rate, shelve, and interact with. The more you use it, the more accurate your recommendations become. We use 14 taste dimensions — pace, tone, depth, and more — to find books that genuinely match how you read.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Profile → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID — make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

My recommendations don't feel right

Rate more books — even 3–4 ratings significantly improve accuracy. You can also:

  • Adjust your taste sliders in Profile → Edit Taste
  • Add books to your Avoid list
  • Use Mood Search to find something specific to how you're feeling right now

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Sign Out, then contact us at the email below and we'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

The app isn't loading / something looks broken

  • Force-quit and reopen the app
  • Check for an update in the App Store
  • If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version

Still need help?

Email us at support@mynextbook.me and we'll get back to you within 48 hours.
