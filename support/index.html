Frequently Asked Questions

How does My Next Book learn my reading taste?

Every time you like, skip, rate, or save a book, My Next Book updates your taste profile across 16 reading dimensions — including pace, tone, emotional intensity, prose style, worldbuilding, and more. During onboarding, you answer 8 questions about your reading preferences, then swipe through 5 books to seed your profile. The more you use the app, the more accurate your recommendations become.

What's included in the free version?

The free version gives you access to your first personalised playlist, Today's Read (daily picks matched to your mood), Mood Search, and your Reading Identity archetype. You can browse the full catalog of over 1 million books and search by mood or text. Pro unlocks unlimited playlist generation, unlimited refinements, taste profile history, and export & share.

How do I start a free trial?

Open My Next Book, choose a subscription tier (Annual, Monthly, or Weekly) from any Pro upgrade screen, and tap "Start my 14-day free trial." Your trial begins immediately. You won't be charged until the 14-day trial ends — and you can cancel anytime from your iPhone's Settings app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel: open Settings on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → My Next Book → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, tap "Restore purchases" on any paywall screen inside the app, or go to the Profile tab → Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app — go to the Profile tab and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes your reading history, taste profile, library, and all associated data. Alternatively, email us at support@mynextbook.me and we'll process your deletion request.

How many books are in each playlist?

Each playlist contains 5 books, curated to your specific reading taste signals. You can refine any playlist to adjust the mix, and each new generation learns from your previous likes, skips, and ratings.

What is Today's Read?

Today's Read is a free daily feature that picks 2–3 books matched to how you're feeling right now. Answer 5 quick questions about your current mood, preferred pace and genre, and get instant picks — no subscription required.

How do I find books in the Catalog?

The Catalog tab shows all your saved books across Want to Read, Reading, Finished, and DNF shelves. To add books, use the Discover tab — search by mood, browse trending picks, or search by title/author. Tap "+ Add to Catalog" on any book to save it.
